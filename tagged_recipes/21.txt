Ingredients

3/4_q cup_u white_m sugar_f
3/4_q cup_u packed_m brown_m sugar_f
1_q 1/2_q cups_u all-purpose_m flour_f
8_q tablespoons_u butter_f
1_q 1/2_q teaspoons_u vanilla_f extract_f
2_q eggs_f
1_q teaspoon_u baking_f soda_f
1/2_q teaspoon_u salt_f
12_q ounces_u semisweet_m chocolate_f chips_f
Directions

Preheat oven to 350_d degrees F_u (175_d degrees C_u).
Melt the butter then stir in the sugars and vanilla.
Combine the flour, baking soda and salt. Stir the flour mixture into the butter mixture. Stir in the beaten eggs. Then add the chocolate chips and stir to combine.
On an ungreased cookie sheet drop cookies about 1 to 2 inches apart. Make the cookies in whatever size you like from small to big. Bake cookies at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 8_t to 10_t minutes_u. The shorter time will produce a chewy cookie, the longer time a crispy cookie.
